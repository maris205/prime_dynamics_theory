# Route update after RH-111

The trace route is no longer forced to pay the worst combinatorial exterior
dimension.  Tail energy gives a rigorous physical concentration upper bound,
and the refined trace certificate reaches the spectral count at `1e-4` and
closes part of the `1e-6` gap.

The remaining gap is not a bookkeeping artifact.  It is caused by the use of
a scalar trace and a global operator tail.  RH-112 should compare direct
exterior perturbation with singular-value products and determine whether a
global wedge-Lipschitz law can propagate four-volume more efficiently or is
an intrinsic dead end.
