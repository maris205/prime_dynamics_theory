- Positive-tail cross-Gram inequality
  `R*R <= ||T|| Q*P*T*P Q`.
- Relative Rayleigh volume theorem with factor `(1-gamma)^4`.
- Scalar tail Gramian as a conservative specialization.
- Equality family `R=-gamma Y` for the multiplicative factor.

## Boundary

generalized eigenvalue remains open.  No Stage A, Hilbert--Polya, zero, or RH
conclusion is claimed.
