# Roadmap after RH-141

The source-interface bottleneck is now quantitative.  The universal RH-140
ball is too large relative to six fourth-mode gaps, while an exact orthogonal
SVD split would cross all ten gates.  The next paper should validate a
quadratic or defect-corrected snapshot radius directly, then propagate the
result through the thresholded packet update.

If the coarsest right-channel margin disappears under outward corrections,
that channel must be isolated or its packet rank changed; it must not be
declared stable from floating eigenvectors alone.
