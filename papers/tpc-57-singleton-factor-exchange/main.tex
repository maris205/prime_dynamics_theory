\noindent\textbf{Keywords:} singleton fiber; rooted collision incidence;
factor exchange; terminal prime; fixed physical shift; occupancy barrier.

\medskip
\noindent\textbf{Convention.}
One nonzero physical shift \(h_0\), one left source system, and one direct
residue/orbit slice are fixed.  Every domain and output energy uses the raw
counting-measure atomic normalization inherited from TPC--53.  The shaped
terminal weight \(\Lambda(n)=(\log n)\Phi_0(n/X)\) is displayed in the
operator and is not the von Mangoldt function.  A \emph{singleton} is
always defined relative to the actually nonzero full terminal support
specified at the point of use.
