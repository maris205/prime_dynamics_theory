# Roadmap after RH-130

## Immediate theorem

Develop the support-restricted generalized Rayleigh theory when both recent
Gram and tail pencils may be singular.  Separate the finite quotient on
`Ran(G)` from the kernel-compatibility condition needed on the full space.

## Natural gauge

Construct a canonical partial-isometry gauge between changing supported
subspaces.  The gauge must be derived from packet geometry, not selected
after the fact solely to minimize the tail factor.

## Affine source law

Use the memory recursion to decompose the next tail into transported old
tail plus a positive birth term.  The new term must cover exactly the
rank-created directions identified here and feed a source-level estimate of
the RH-123 coefficient `q_n`.

## Boundary

The local four-directional packet remains viable on the frozen data, but the
purely multiplicative cross-scale route is closed.  Do not reuse the RH-125
24/24 regularized count as evidence for a floor-free all-level chain.
