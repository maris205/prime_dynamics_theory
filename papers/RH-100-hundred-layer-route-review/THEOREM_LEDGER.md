## Review theorem

For a monotone AND/OR dependency formula whose leaves are closed, open, or
red, minimal completion bundles are computed recursively:

- closed leaf -> empty bundle;
- open leaf -> singleton gate;
- red leaf -> no viable bundle;
- OR -> antichain union;
- AND -> antichain of Cartesian unions.

This conservatively substitutes decomposed subroutes without treating finite
validation as an all-level closure.
