\noindent\textbf{Convention.}
Every atomic energy in the selection statements is the
actual-coefficient-weighted carrier energy on retained pre-terminal
labels.  The unseparated physical-profile norm and a grouped output are
named separately.  The shift \(h_0\ne0\) is fixed.  Unit phases are never
converted into magnitude information, and the many-to-one grouping
\(s=d(m)r\) is never moved through a squared norm.
