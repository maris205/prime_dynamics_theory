## Validation

- 360 threshold-labelled records and 322 genuinely supported records.
- All 322 supported records receive adaptive certificates.
- Maximum first-passage depths `6`, `5`, and `4` at thresholds `1e-8`,
  `1e-6`, and `1e-4`.
- Zero tail, dominance, monotonicity, minimality, and completeness failures.

## Boundary

scales.  It proves no all-level physical support law, uniform Stage A,
Hilbert--Polya operator, zero identification, or Riemann Hypothesis result.
