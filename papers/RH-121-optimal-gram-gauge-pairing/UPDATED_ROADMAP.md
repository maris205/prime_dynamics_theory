# Roadmap after RH-121

The exact-Gram minimax problem is closed, but recent-Gram alignment alone is
not a uniformly controlled physical mechanism on the finite chain.

1. Prove a sharp fixed-coordinate obstruction: even identical relative
   spectra can make the no-gauge constants diverge.
2. Replace exact comparison by a defect-stable recurrence that can absorb
   outward transport radii.
3. Transport leading normalization and three-mode capacity separately.
4. Combine only constants certified on one common assembly path.
