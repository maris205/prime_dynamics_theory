# Roadmap after RH-137

## New distinction

Long-run contractivity is not required to cross an isolated transition.
Thirty-one of the 33 RH-136 walls are finite-horizon viable because the
incoming certified tail is far below the exact safety radius.

## Remaining finite obstruction

Two coarse left-channel transitions have birth forcing above one and actual
relative tail above one.  No gauge acting only on the old frame can repair
that target-frame birth slice.  A larger packet, reset, or delayed start is
required if those chains are to be retained.

zeta-zero identification, or RH theorem.
