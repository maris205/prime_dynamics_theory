- Exact pointwise Young envelope `q + (sqrt(Ax)+sqrt(B))^2`.
- Sharp finite-step safety radius and explicit contractive fixed point.
- Greedy finite-horizon optimality for monotone finite candidate families.
- Gauge-independent obstruction when the birth term is already superunit.

- 330 transitions and 30 chains at 80 decimal digits; zero dominance failures.
- 31/33 long-run contractivity walls crossed safely.

- Euclidean polar alone: 318 safe transitions and 21 safe chains.

## Not proved

- Stage A, Hilbert--Polya, zeta-zero, or RH results.
