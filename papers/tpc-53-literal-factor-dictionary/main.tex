\noindent\textbf{Convention.}

otherwise.  The literal static mask is never replaced by a signed
rank-one representation.  Every norm called ``atomic'' below is the
retained-label pre-terminal norm; it is not the terminal residual norm or
the native affine physical Gram.  Constants may depend on the fixed
cutoffs and residue moduli but not on \(X\).
