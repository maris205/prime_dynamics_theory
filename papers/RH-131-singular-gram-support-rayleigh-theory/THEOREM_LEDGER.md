# Theorem ledger

## Proved

- Full-space finite Rayleigh control iff `ker(G) subset ker(D)`.
- Sharp support quotient from `G^{dagger/2} D G^{dagger/2}`.
- Sharp rank-`r` pseudovolume lower bound `(1-gamma)^r sqrt(pdet G)`.
- Arbitrarily small kernel leakage still makes the full quotient infinite.
- Outward compressed squared-Rayleigh correction
  `q + (r_D + q r_G)/(g_hat-r_G)`.

- 4,096 compatible synthetic cases, zero failures.
- 1,024 kernel-leakage cases, all detected.
- 54 zero-tail and 66 full-tail RH-130 states.
- 24 rank-creating RH-130 edges, exactly matching 24 infinite factors.
