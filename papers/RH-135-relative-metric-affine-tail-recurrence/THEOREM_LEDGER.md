# Theorem ledger

## Proved

- Exact relative coefficients
  `rho = r ||G^(1/2) O G'^(-1/2)||^2` and
  `q = ||G'^(-1/2) F G'^(-1/2)||`.
- Sharp vanishing-absolute-forcing and raw-contraction obstructions.
- A contractive Young parameter exists iff the metric-decay base is below 1.
- The optimized fixed-floor objective `q/(1-rho)`.

## Audited

- 330 recurrences, zero failures.
- 90 zero-tail, 24 first-birth, and 216 recurrent transitions.
- 51 recurrent transitions contractive-feasible; 165 metric-blocked.
- All 75 nontrivial feasible transitions have fixed floor below `4.7e-6`.

## Not proved

- A metric-aware gauge with uniform subunit recurrence.
- Cross-scale recurrence, Stage A, Hilbert–Polya, zeta-zero, or RH results.
